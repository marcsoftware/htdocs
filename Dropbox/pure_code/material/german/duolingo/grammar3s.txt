`
Furthermore, wohin is separable into wo + hin:

    [Wo:Wo:wohin:woher] ist mein Schuh hin? (Where did my shoe go?){41,table41}
`
The same goes for woher (where from):

    [Woher:Wo:wohin:woher] kommst du? (Where are you from){41,table41}
`
might become

    [Wo:Wo:wohin:woher] kommst du (her)?{41,table41}
