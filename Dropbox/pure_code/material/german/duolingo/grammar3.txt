//Nicht

Negating a noun that has a 
definite article like der Junge (the boy) 
That is not the boy.
Das ist [nicht] der Junge. 
    
`
Negating a noun that has a 
possessive pronoun like mein Glas (my glass) 
Das ist [nicht] mein Glas. (That is not my glass).
`    
Negating the verb:
I do not drink.
Ich trinke [nicht]. 
`    
Negating an adverb or adverbial phrase. 
I do not dance often.
Ich tanze [nicht] oft. 
`    
Negating an adjective that is used with sein (to be):
I am not hungry.
Ich bin [nicht] hungrig .

//41


    [Wo:wohin:woher] ist mein Schuh? (Where is my shoe?)

    [Wohin:Wo:wohin:woher] gehst du? (Where are you going (to)?)

Furthermore, wohin is separable into wo + hin:

    [Wo:Wo:wohin:woher] ist mein Schuh hin? (Where did my shoe go?)

The same goes for woher (where from):

    [Woher:Wo:wohin:woher] kommst du? (Where are you from)

might become

    [Wo:Wo:wohin:woher] kommst du (her)?
