//Nicht

    Negating a noun that has a definite article like der Junge (the boy) 
    That is not the boy.
    Das ist [nicht] der Junge. 
    
`

    Negating a noun that has a possessive pronoun like mein Glas (my glass) 
     Das ist [nicht] mein Glas. (That is not my glass).
`    
    Negating the verb:
    I do not drink.
     Ich trinke [nicht]. 
`    
    Negating an adverb or adverbial phrase. 
    I do not dance often.
     Ich tanze [nicht] oft. 
`    
    Negating an adjective that is used with sein (to be):
     I am not hungry.
     Ich bin [nicht] hungrig .
`
